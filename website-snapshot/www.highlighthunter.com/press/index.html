Praise for Highlight Hunter


Highlight Hunter is invaluable to us at GoPro. Our video producers save hours every week because we can get to the interesting footage quickly and spend more time in the fun, creative part of production. – Kyle C., GoPro video production team

The problem is that sifting through those hours of footage to find the gems is time-consuming and a pain in the ass. That’s why Noah Spitzer-Williams and team founded Highlight Hunter, a free app for Mac and PC that aims to cut the hours you spend scanning footage down to minutes. – Rip Empson

Enter Highlight Hunter, a Mac (and PC) app which runs tirelessly through any amount of video and separates out the highlights into discrete 30-second clips, ready for further editing. – Charlie Sorrel

If you’re an outdoor adventurer who shoots video while skiing, snowboarding or mountain biking, Highlight Hunter might just save you a few hours when you get back in front of your computer. And that means you can devote even more time to your favorite pursuits. – John Cook

Highlight Hunter, a new free app for Mac and PC users, is making a mark on the video scene with its ability to cutting video editing time in half. – Kelli Hargrove

Editing got you down? Highlight Hunter is looking to make editing certain projects almost 100% faster. You may not be using Highlight Hunter on your next feature film, but there are fantastic ideas at work with this brand new app. – Joe Marine

Video producers who regularly find themselves searching through a business presentation or similar live event for the one or two moments worth salvaging will also likely find Highlight Hunter a nice little time saver. – Darren Quick

Editing long streams of video can almost take the fun out of using your helmet cam as you spend hours and hours trying to pin point the awesome shots out of your full day’s adventure. Well, we’ve found an awesome solution to this problem! Our friends at Highlight Hunter have developed software to alleviate this problem altogether. The best ideas in the world are the simplest!

We want content, we want speed, and we want to see it now. Highlight Hunter founder, Noah Spitzer-Williams, realized just that. – Jenna Hannon

It will not actually fix any backlog issue you have but going forward could tremendously ease the task of finding the highlights in the video you are shooting at to edit it much faster, by as much as 8x according to the company. – Greg Quick

One of the reasons I don’t like video is I don’t have the time or patience to go through a full days shooting to find the highlights, to cut them out into individual files and do all the re-editing stuff involved with it. Highlighter Hunter may go some way to alleviating this concern. – Ian Lim

Damn that is freakin’ clever. That’s a very simple solution for quite a complex problem! – Roy Sahachaisere

You know what a giant pain in the butt it can be when you shoot a ton of footage on your GoPro. Long periods of nothing much happening with a few seconds of action that you have to go searching for. Highlight Hunter aims to solve this problem by searching through your footage for black spots that you create by holding your hand over the camera lens after a highlight occurs.
