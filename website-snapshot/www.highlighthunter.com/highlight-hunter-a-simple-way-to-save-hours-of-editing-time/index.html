Highlight Hunter – A Simple Way to Save Hours of Video Editing Time

24 Oct 2012 |  Posted by Noah |  0 Comment.
0

We’re really excited to launch the Highlight Hunter Blog today. Highlight Hunter helps camera owners quickly find and share the highlights in their videos.

The reason we built Highlight Hunter is simple — recording is easy but finding the interesting moments to share is hard.

A little back story:

A few years ago my buddies and I began snowboarding with sports cameras because they make it easy to capture our best tricks and epic wipeouts.

We quickly learned that sports videography is unique because you never know when something exciting is going to happen. My buddy could take a big spill at any moment and I’d hate myself if the camera wasn’t recording. Since we never wanted to miss any unexpected moments, we got in the habit of never turning off the camera.

But this created a new problem! Every day we went riding, we would come home with several hours of footage to filter through. Oftentimes times we never even watched what we recorded because it was too tedious to find the fun parts.

A few beers and brainstorms later, the concept for Highlight Hunter was born:

  1. Record like you normally do, but after something cool happens, cover the camera lens for a second to mark the moment.
  2. Drop your footage into Highlight Hunter and it creates a set of highlights–one for each time you covered the lens.
  3. Trim the ones you like and share them with a single click.

    4. Now we all have more time to get back outside and play!
